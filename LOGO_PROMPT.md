# Logo Prompts (Google Imagegen 3)

Five logo concepts for Hyper Fingerprints. All prompts target a clean, minimal, scientific aesthetic.

**Shared settings:** Square 1:1 aspect ratio, transparent or white background, flat vector style, no gradients unless noted. Primary color: KIT green (#009682).

---

## 1. Benzene Fingerprint

A minimal benzene hexagon ring in KIT green (#009682) where three alternating edges dissolve into parallel horizontal dashes resembling a barcode or binary fingerprint. Clean flat vector logo, single color, white background, no text. Scientific and geometric, suitable as a favicon.

## 2. Molecule to Vector Arrow

A simple three-atom molecular structure (two circles connected by lines in a V-shape) on the left, with a clean right-pointing arrow leading to a small square grid of filled and empty cells representing a vector. All rendered as thin line art in KIT green (#009682) on white background. Minimal, geometric, flat vector logo, no text.

## 3. Hexagonal Waveform

A single hexagon outline in KIT green (#009682) with a sine wave passing horizontally through its center, representing the Fourier transform at the heart of HRR encoding. Clean flat vector logo, single color, white background, no text. Minimal and abstract, works at small sizes.

## 4. Atom Orbital Grid

A stylized atom symbol — a single filled circle with two thin elliptical orbits — where the orbits are formed by evenly spaced small dots rather than solid lines, evoking both atomic structure and high-dimensional data points. KIT green (#009682) on white background. Clean flat vector logo, no text, minimal.

## 5. Molecular Hash

A fingerprint whorl pattern (like a human fingerprint) where the curved ridges are replaced by thin lines connecting small circular nodes, forming a molecular-graph-like topology. KIT green (#009682), single color, white background, flat vector logo, no text. The intersection of fingerprints and molecular graphs in one symbol.

## 6. Hypercube Molecule

A wireframe tesseract (4D hypercube projection) in KIT green (#009682) where the inner and outer cubes are connected at their vertices by thin lines. Several vertices are highlighted as slightly larger filled circles, resembling atoms in a molecular graph embedded in higher-dimensional space. Clean flat vector logo, single color, white background, no text. Geometric and mathematical.

## 7. Dimensional Explosion

A small benzene hexagon on the left that fans outward into dozens of thin radiating lines spreading in all directions, forming a starburst or dandelion-like pattern — visualizing a low-dimensional molecule being projected into a high-dimensional vector space. KIT green (#009682), single color, white background, flat vector logo, no text. Dynamic yet minimal.

## 8. Layered Molecular Rings

Three concentric hexagon rings of increasing size, each slightly rotated relative to the others, with small circular nodes at the vertices. The layering evokes multiple dimensions and message-passing depth, while the hexagons reference molecular structure. KIT green (#009682), single color, white background, flat vector logo, no text.

## 9. Fingerprint Hypersphere

A circle composed of many thin parallel curved lines (like a fingerprint cross-section) intersected by a perpendicular set of curved lines at a different angle, forming a sphere-like grid pattern. Small molecular nodes are placed at a few intersections. Represents a fingerprint mapped onto a high-dimensional hypersphere. KIT green (#009682), single color, white background, flat vector logo, no text.

## 10. Molecular Constellation

A molecular graph (5-6 nodes connected by edges) where each node emits thin dotted lines extending outward in many directions like coordinate axes, suggesting each atom exists in a high-dimensional feature space. The dotted projection lines fade with distance. KIT green (#009682), single color, white background, flat vector logo, no text. Clean and scientific.
